title: Mixture of Conditional Probability Spaces defined by Hausdorff measures
authors:
- Serena Doria
- Erya Yang
date: '2025-12-10'

summary: We study stochastic choice rules satisfying a generalized version of the independence of irrelevant
alternatives using conditional probability spaces defined by Hausdorff measures and M¨obius transform.
We develop a general framework for mixtures of such conditional probability spaces on arbitrary domains,
identify conditions under which such mixtures remain conditional probability spaces (i.e., satisfy the
chain rule), and show, in particular, that mixture representations that still satisfy the chain rule are
highly restrictive
